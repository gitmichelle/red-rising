Meet Pierce

Pierce Brown spent his childhood building forts and setting traps for cousins in the woods of six states and the deserts of two. Graduating from college in 2010, he fancied the idea of continuing his studies at Hogwarts. Unfortunately, he doesn’t have a magical bone in his body. Instead, he tried to make it as a writer. He lives Los Angeles, where he scribbles tales of spaceships, wizards, ghouls, and most things old or bizarre.
